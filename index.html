About Me

I am a Master's student in Computer Science at the University of Tehran with a focus on Artificial Intelligence.

My research interests lie at the intersection of Human-Computer Interaction (HCI), Human-Robot Interaction (HRI), and Machine Learning. I am driven by the goal of building data-driven systems that genuinely help people in their everyday lives. This has led me to join the Human and Robot Interaction Lab (TaarLab), where I've worked on challenges in robotic scene rearrangement. Concurrently, under the guidance of Prof. B. Babaali , I have also explored Human Activity Recognition (HAR) , applying time series foundation models as part of a team competing in the SHL 2025 Challenge.
